Forest scientist and quantitative analyst specializing in forest biometrics, carbon dynamics, and silvicultural management. Experienced in both running various lab assays and conducting forest inventory measurements, including FIA-style sampling, vegetation surveys, soil sampling, and ecosystem carbon monitoring across diverse forest ecosystems in North America. Proficient in statistical modeling, growth and yield analysis, forest carbon estimation, and large-scale data analytics using R and Python. Skilled at integrating field observations, inventory datasets, and ecological measurements to generate actionable insights that support sustainable forest management, carbon accounting, and natural resource decision-making. Passionate about applying quantitative methods to address complex challenges in forestry, biomass utilization, and climate mitigation while effectively communicating technical findings to diverse audiences.
