Forest scientist specializing in regeneration ecology, climate-adaptive silviculture, and forest carbon dynamics. Experienced in field research, growth and yield modeling, FIA-style inventories, and fire ecology. Adept at mentoring students from diverse backgrounds and supporting inclusive learning environments. Committed to effective teaching, student mentorship, and field-based research that supports resilient and sustainable forest regeneration of northern hardwood, boreal transition, and pine ecosystems in Minnesota and the Great Lakes region.
